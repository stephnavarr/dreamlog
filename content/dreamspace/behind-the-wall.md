---
title: "behind the walls"
date: 2022-03-17
excerpt: "This poem is about attachment patterns, but I didn't know that term at the time I wrote this. The garden represents my inner, emotional world."
---

little woman,
big girl.

the garden i grow is a home you'll never see;
a world you'll never know.

I dream of it
the day our hands touch again.
I'll show you all the fruit I grew out of my tears
and you'll pick me up 
and we'll run across the world
leaving my garden 
to rot.

we'll come back
you'll drop me off at the gate 
and never return.
I'll plant my seeds again 
and wait for you.

one day I'll lock the gate,
close the doors,
turn off the lights.

for now I choose to feel you,
even if it hurts.
