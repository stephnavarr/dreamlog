title: "untitled 01"
date: 2023-06-17
excerpt: "Inspired by Jung, this poem is about the Shadow, the Self, and the Child integrating into one. My adult self dreams about wholeness, but my child self just wants someone to stay."
---

maybe we'll all dance one day
bare feet on dewy grass-
my pains
my sunshines
my strengths
my weaknesses-
maybe one day we'll creep out of the shadows
and show the world our faces.
the light and the dark.
she'll meet us at that place
take us into her center.

hug me,
hold me here
look at me again
tell me that the things I've made are beautiful
tell me that you remember me
tell me that you see me.
tell me you won't go away again
that you love me
and we can share the world again.

there'll be no lines anymore
the Earth is mine
the Earth is ours.
