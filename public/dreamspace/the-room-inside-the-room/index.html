January 15, 2024

the room that lives inside the room

depression is a state I know well

there is a room
inside my chest

it has no windows.

I go there when
the world brings its rain.

there are no clocks- only the sound
of something old
whispering you were here before
you will be here after

I do not always come back
changed
but I always come back
knowing

the room is not an ending
it is the place
where I am most
exactly
whatever I am now.
